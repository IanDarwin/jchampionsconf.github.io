Call for Proposals

Submit a Talk Today

The jChampions Conference 2020 call for proposals is now open !

Before submitting, please review these guidelines. Only Java Champions can submit talks for this conference. However, anyone can attend. We will be accepting 1 hour talks or 2 hour workshop proposals.

The conference will be held virtually in January 2021 on Wednesday 1/13. Thusday 1/14, Monday 1/18 and Tuesday 1/19.

CFP DEADLINE: Monday September 21

Notifications for acceptance will go out on a rolling basis; submit early and often!

Rejection notices will go out on October 1 or earlier.

We look forward to reviewing your submissions!

PLEASE TAKE 30 SECONDS TO REVIEW THESE GUIDELINES!

CFP Instructions

  • Once you've signed up on the CFP site, click 'Create New Event'. Make sure you also mark your availability.
  • Select the track accordingly in the CFP system.
  • Presentations (lectures) are 60 mins. This includes question time. Workshops are 120 mins.
  • Please email jchampionsconf@gmail.com if you have any questions.
